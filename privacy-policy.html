By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

When you submit the Contact form on the Site, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Company name (optional)
  • The service vertical you're interested in (Finance & Advisory, Technology, Agriculture, etc.)
  • The message you write

You may also choose to contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email using the links on the Site — those channels are governed by the privacy practices of WhatsApp/your email provider in addition to this Policy for anything you send us directly.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • IP address — looked up via a third-party service (api.ipify.org) at the moment you submit the Contact form, and used solely to apply a submission rate limit that protects the form against spam and abuse.
  • Source page — the page the form was submitted from.
  • Standard hosting/CDN logs — our hosting providers (GitHub Pages and/or Cloudflare Pages) and our security provider (Cloudflare) automatically log requests to the Site (e.g. IP address, browser type, timestamps, pages requested) as a normal part of serving and securing any website. We do not directly access or search these logs for individual visitors; they exist at the infrastructure level for security, performance, and abuse-prevention purposes.

3.3 Cookies

The Site itself does not set advertising or analytics cookies. However, the following embedded third-party services may set their own cookies when their component loads on a page you visit:

  • Cloudflare Turnstile (Contact page) — sets a short-lived cookie as part of verifying you're not a bot when submitting the form.
  • Google Maps (Contact page) — the embedded map may set cookies per Google's own cookie policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads font files from Google's servers; Google may log the request per its own privacy practices.

These are set directly by the third party in your browser, not by us, and are governed by that third party's own privacy policy (linked in Section 5).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry and communicate with you about our services.
  • To maintain a record of enquiries for our own business purposes.
  • To detect, prevent, and address spam, abuse, or security issues on the Contact form.
  • To comply with applicable law.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising or third-party marketing.

We retain Contact form submissions for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry and for our legitimate business record-keeping, unless you request earlier deletion (see Section 8). Automatically-collected hosting/CDN logs are retained per each infrastructure provider's own standard retention schedule, which we do not control.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have some or all of the following rights over your personal information. Contact us at contact@xsynva.com to exercise any of these.

8.1 If you are in India

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 (including the SPDI Rules, 2011), you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, seek correction or erasure of inaccurate or unnecessary data, withdraw consent at any time, and file a grievance with our Grievance Officer (Section 10) if you're unsatisfied with how we've handled a request.

8.2 If you are in the European Union / EEA / UK

Under the GDPR / UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8.3 If you are in California, USA

Under the CCPA/CPRA, you have the right to know what personal information we've collected, request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising your rights. As noted above, we do not sell personal information.

8.4 Everyone else

Regardless of location, you may always ask us what information we hold about you and request that we correct or delete it, and we will respond in good faith consistent with applicable law and our legitimate business needs (e.g. financial/legal record-keeping obligations).

9. Data Security

We rely on industry-standard safeguards provided by our infrastructure partners — HTTPS encryption in transit (via Cloudflare/GitHub Pages), Cloudflare Turnstile and IP-based rate limiting to reduce spam/abuse of the Contact form, and Google's account-level security for the underlying Sheet. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
