Our Services

Three ways we work with clients.

Each engagement starts with the same question: what does this business actually need to be true about its finances to reach its next stage?

Domestic Taxation

Structuring, forecasting and capital planning grounded in the realities of your sector — not generic templates.

  • Cash flow and capital structuring
  • Financial forecasting and modeling

Business Consulting

We step into the operating model itself — not just the numbers around it — when a business is restructuring for its next stage. That means looking at how decisions actually get made, where approvals slow down, and which processes exist on paper but not in practice.

  • Operating model design and org structure review
  • Process mapping and governance documentation
  • Decision-rights and approval-chain clean-up

Investment Enablement

When a business is heading into a raise, the gap is rarely the story — it's whether the numbers behind the story can survive diligence. We build the data room, the financial narrative and the supporting model so investors can move quickly instead of stalling on requests for documentation that doesn't exist yet.

  • Data room build and diligence preparation
  • Investor-ready financial narrative and model
  • Cap table and term-sheet review support
