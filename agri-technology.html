Agriculture — Farm Technology

Precision tools for the field, and a platform for procurement.

On top of our 15-acre farm, we're piloting precision-farming services with select partners and building XDairy — our dairy procurement and quality platform — with AI-assisted features designed in from day one.

Farm-Tech Pilots — Early Stage XDairy — In Development
Precision Farming — Pilot

Field-level work, starting small on purpose.

Alongside the farm, we're piloting a small set of farm-tech services with select partners before offering them more broadly.

Precision Spraying (Pilot)

Aerial application trials aimed at cutting input waste versus manual spraying, running with a small group of partner farms.

Farm Digitization

Moving plot records and input history from paper registers into a simple digital system, one farm at a time.

Resource Planning

Early-stage tools to plan water and input use against actual field need rather than a fixed calendar.

AI & Machine Learning

AI, built into the field and the ledger.

The same AI-assisted approach we use across Xsynva applies here — from herd health to milk quality to farmer support.

Predictive Flagging

Early flags for crop stress and herd or milk-quality anomalies, drawn from field and farm sensor data.

Automated Reporting

Raw flight, field and procurement data turned into reports farmers and cooperative managers can act on.

AI-Assisted Support

Chat and WhatsApp support built into how farmers and partners reach us, day to day.

Why We Started Here

Dairy procurement, because the need was clearest.

Running our own dairy farm made the procurement and quality problem concrete — daily transactions, real quality variance, and a farmer network that needed better tools. XDairy is the result, and the rest of our farm technology follows once each pilot is proven.

XDairy — Dairy Management Platform

XDairy: procurement and quality, in one ledger.

Built for dairies and cooperatives managing daily collections across a farmer network — connecting the collection point to the business ledger without manual reconciliation.

  • Milk collection tracking by route and shift
  • Procurement management with rate-card automation
  • Farmer network management and payouts
  • Quality monitoring — fat, SNF, adulteration flags
