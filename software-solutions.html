Software Solutions — In Build

AI-assisted software platforms for fleet operations and agriculture.

Two platforms, one engineering team: a drone fleet management system for operators running aircraft across sites, and XDairy, our AI-assisted dairy procurement platform for farmer networks.

Fleet platform in active development XDairy in pilot
Our Approach

AI is designed in from day one, not bolted on.

Every platform we build starts with the assumption that prediction, anomaly detection and automated reporting are part of the core product — not a feature we add once the basics work. That's true whether the data is coming from a drone's flight telemetry or a dairy collection route, which is why both platforms below share the same underlying AI layer.

AI-Assisted Fleet Operations

Command every aircraft from one screen.

A single operating system for organizations running drones across multiple sites — built for the people who plan missions, fly them, and answer for the results.

  • Fleet monitoring with live location and battery status
  • Mission planning with geofencing and route templates
  • Maintenance tracking tied to flight-hour thresholds
  • Compliance reporting aligned to airspace regulation

Why We Started Here

Dairy procurement, because the need was clearest.

Running our own dairy farm made the procurement and quality problem concrete — daily transactions, real quality variance, and a farmer network that needed better tools. XDairy is the result, and the rest of our farm technology follows once each pilot is proven.

AI-Assisted Dairy Procurement

XDairy — procurement and quality, in one ledger.

Our agriculture software arm. XDairy is built for dairies and cooperatives managing daily collections across a farmer network — connecting the collection point to the business ledger, with AI flags for quality and payout anomalies along the way.

  • Milk collection tracking by route and shift
  • Procurement management with rate-card automation
  • Farmer network management and payouts
  • AI-flagged quality monitoring — fat, SNF, adulteration
